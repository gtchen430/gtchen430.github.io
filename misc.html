Motorsports

 

I am a car enthusiast with a keen interest in kart racing and High Performance Driving Events (HPDE, often referred to as track days). Don't get me wrong, I don't burn tires, have a loud exhaust, or engage in illegal street racing. My passion lies in driving on race tracks and refining my racing techniques. If you share a love for cars and have an interest in race tracks, especially in the Bay or Research Triangle area, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Additionally, I possess extensive knowledge about car maintenance and the intricacies of acquiring used legacy cars. If you have any car-related queries, feel free to ask!

Music

I have a deep appreciation for music, with classical music holding a special place in my heart. I play both the piano and harpsichord from time to time, and I'm always eager to expand my knowledge in music theory.
